Resume Screening Logic

How AI evaluates candidate fit for positions

How This Works

Automated resume screening follows a structured evaluation process:

  • Keyword Extraction: Identify relevant skills and technologies from both job description and resume
  • Semantic Matching: Account for variations (e.g., "Data Analytics" matches "Data Analysis")
  • Scoring Algorithm: Calculate match percentage based on required vs present skills
  • Gap Analysis: Highlight missing critical competencies

Real-World Application: Production systems use NLP models trained on thousands of job descriptions and resumes, learning industry-specific terminology and skill relationships. This accelerates initial screening while reducing unconscious bias.
